Liyu Menged Charity Association was founded by volunteers/honest hearted young people in Hosaina city and the association got its legal status on July 7, 2013.
The purpose of the association.It works to identify and help mothers who have homes in our city and have no claimants.It is an association that has a plan to support those who have not found the opportunity to work and have a difficult life.The association is structured as a partnership and has more than 50 members.
Among the works that the association intends to do.To provide support for elderly people who cannot afford and cannot work.Creating job opportunities for those who are able to workIt is an association that has plans to renovate their houses and similar schemes for those whose houses are leaking and cannot renovate by talking to the government.
